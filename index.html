Bing Liu

Ph.D. candidate at Southeast University, Nanjing, China.

I am a second-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Southeast University, advised by Prof. Guilin Qi. My research interests mainly lie in Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning.

Recently, I am focusing on Information Extraction (IE) from unstructured text. Specifically, I am interested in designing IE algorithms that do not suffer from human annotation dependence.
